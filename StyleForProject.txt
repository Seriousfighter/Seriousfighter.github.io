Colors:

Background: bg-gray-900 or bg-black for dark backgrounds.
Primary Button: Use bg-green-500 for active buttons with hover:bg-green-600 for hover effects.
Text:
Headings: text-white for contrast against dark backgrounds.
Subtext: text-gray-400 for secondary text or descriptions.
Card Backgrounds: Use bg-gray-800 with slight transparency like bg-gray-800/70 for overlays.
Accent Colors: Use text-yellow-500 or text-green-400 for highlighted elements.
Typography:

Font Family: Use font-sans with a clean, modern look.
Font Sizes:
Headings: text-4xl for main headings, text-2xl for section headings.
Subheadings: text-lg with font-medium.
Body Text: text-sm md:text-base for readability.
Buttons:

Default Button: bg-green-500 text-white font-bold py-3 px-6 rounded-lg hover:bg-green-600 transition-all.
Secondary Button: bg-white text-black border border-gray-300 font-medium py-3 px-6 rounded-lg hover:bg-gray-200 transition-all.
Spacing & Layout:

Use py-8 or py-16 for section padding.
px-4 for mobile spacing, with md:px-8 for desktop.
Flexbox Utilities: flex items-center justify-center for centering elements.
